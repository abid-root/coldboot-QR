Privacy Policy

Privacy-first by default.

FlowSync is a browser-based static QR code generator. The current version does not require an account, database, backend, or cloud storage.

What we do not collect

  • We do not store your QR text, links, Wi-Fi details, contact details, or location data.
  • We do not store uploaded logos or generated QR images.
  • We do not require login or personal account information.

How the tool works

QR generation happens inside your browser. When you type content into the tool, it is used to generate the QR code on your device.

Local storage

FlowSync may use your browser local storage only for interface preferences, such as theme mode. This helps the site remember your selected appearance.

Static QR codes

Static QR codes cannot be edited after download. We also do not track scans, devices, locations, or analytics in the current version.
