FlowSync QR Template Pack (fs-tamplate)

What this contains:
- 20 standalone editable QR card templates.
- Each template is its own folder with one index.html file.
- HTML, CSS, and JavaScript are together inside each template file.
- No backend and no external library required.

How to use a template:
1. Create your QR on your main FlowSync page.
2. Download the QR as PNG.
3. Open fs-tamplate/templates/index.html.
4. Choose a template.
5. Upload your QR PNG.
6. Click any text on the card or edit from the right panel.
7. Click Download PNG.

Install into your site:
1. Unzip fs-tamplate.zip in your project root.
2. Run this from the project root:
   powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File .s-tamplate\install.ps1
