QR types

Which one should you use?

  • URL: website, portfolio, menu, product page.
  • Text: simple message or offline note.
  • Wi-Fi: share network name and password.
  • vCard: business contact sharing.
  • Location: map pin or physical place.
Scan safety

Keep it readable

  • Keep strong contrast between QR and background.
  • Do not make the logo too large.
  • Use enough padding around the QR code.
  • Use SVG for print or design work.
  • Test after changing colors.
